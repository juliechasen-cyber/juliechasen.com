Three release types, one wire format

Pick the moment. The tool handles the structure.

01 — Coming Soon

Pre-market whisper release

For high-profile, generational, or unique assets going out before official marketing materials circulate. Pricing stays private by default; the distinguishing fact leads.

02 — Just Listed

Exclusive listing announcement

The workhorse. Front-loads the news and the numbers; bounded superlatives only when the facts back them up. Ready to send to trade press.

03 — Just Sold

Closed transaction release

Celebrates execution and proves the track record. Missing facts (buyer, closing date) become bracketed slots — never invented, always flagged for a disclosure check.

Why I built this

A gift to the industry — and a preview of what I'm building next.

I spent years inside one of the country's largest CRE brokerages watching how much time — and how many missed launch windows — went into press releases that never got written. The wire format is well-defined; the bottleneck is drafting.

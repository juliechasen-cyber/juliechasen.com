Why custom matters

Every dollar in fees is a dollar not in the mission.

Platform fees saved
2.9%
On every transaction, forever.

Most donation platforms — Donorbox, Givebutter, Classy — layer their own 2.9% platform fee on top of payment processing. For a nonprofit moving real volume, that's thousands of dollars a year that never reach the cause.

Going direct to Stripe with a custom-built form cuts that layer out completely. Same secure checkout. Same trusted payment processor. None of the middle-platform overhead.

It's not just about saving money. It's about respect for every donor's intent — when someone gives $100, all $100 should go to the work.
